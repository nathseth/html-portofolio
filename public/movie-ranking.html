The Best Movie According to Jonathan

My top 3 movies of all-time

God's Crooked Lines

A private detective who claims to be suffering from paranoia checks into a psychiatric hospital to investigate another patient's mysterious death.

Midsommar

A young American couple, their relationship foundering, travel to a fabled Swedish midsummer festival where a seemingly pastoral paradise transforms into a sinister, dread-soaked nightmare as the locals reveal their terrifying agenda.

The Invisible Guest

A young businessman wakes up in a locked hotel room next to the body of his dead lover. He hires a prestigious lawyer to defend him, and over the course of one night, they work together to find out what happened.
